PDF Compression Tips

Image-Heavy PDFs

For PDFs with many images, use High or Extreme compression with lower DPI settings for maximum size reduction.

Text Documents

Text-based PDFs compress well with Medium compression while maintaining readability.

Email Sharing

Use 150 DPI and Medium compression for the best balance of size and quality for email attachments.

Print Quality

Keep 300 DPI and Low compression if the PDF will be printed or requires highest quality.
